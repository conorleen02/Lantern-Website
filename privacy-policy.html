Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 4, 2025
Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Lantern Intelligence Inc. ("Lantern," "we," "our," or "us") provides demand-forecasting software and related services to wholesale distributors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, www.lanternhq.com ("Site"). It does not cover data processed through our software platform, which is governed by customer contracts and data-processing agreements.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information from visitors to our website.

a. Information you provide

When you fill out a form, request a demo, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name and role
  • Any message or details you include

b. Information collected automatically

When you browse our Site, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and general location (city, region)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and referring website
  • Cookie and analytics data (via Google Analytics if enabled in the future)

c. Information from third-party tools

We may receive data through tools we use to operate our website and communicate with visitors, such as:

  • Attio (contact forms, communications, and customer management)
  • Google Analytics (website usage metrics)
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP) (hosting and security infrastructure)

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and demo requests
  • Communicate with you about our products or updates
  • Improve the content and performance of our Site
  • Analyze traffic and visitor interactions
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. How We Share Information

We only share personal data with trusted service providers who help us run our website or communicate with you, such as:

  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP) for hosting
  • Attio for contact management and communication
  • Google Analytics for usage insights

These providers are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations. We do not sell or disclose information to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our Site and understand how visitors interact with it.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Keep the website secure and functional
  • Measure and improve performance
  • Understand visitor behavior and traffic patterns

We use Google Analytics, which places cookies on your device to collect information such as your IP address, device type, browser, and pages visited. This information helps us analyze how our Site is used so we can improve it. Google Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify individual visitors.

You can learn more about how Google Analytics works and how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

If you prefer not to be tracked, you can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the browser add-on available at: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

You can also control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features or functionality of the Site.

5. Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. We use industry-standard security practices — including HTTPS encryption and access controls — to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Ask that we correct or delete your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To make a request, email info@lanternhq.com. We will verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is intended for business professionals and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated by updating the policy on our Site.
